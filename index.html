AIIMS Geriatrics

Healthcare • Video Marketing • Social Media Strategy

The Challenge

AIIMS Geriatrics faced challenges in expanding its digital presence and educating the public about geriatric health issues. They needed to enhance patient engagement, particularly with elderly patients and their caregivers.

The Solution

We developed a comprehensive video marketing strategy, producing tailored content to engage the older demographic. By utilizing targeted social media, we boosted patient education and outreach.

The Impact

Our strategic efforts resulted in a 400% increase in video engagement and a 2x growth in social media following. These outcomes helped drive patient consultations and raise awareness.
